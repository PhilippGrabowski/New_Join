What is Join?

Join is a project management and workflow system. It uses a visual board with columns representing different stages of work to provide a clear view of the workflow. Work items are represented as cards and their movement across the board indicates progress. Join focuses on limiting work in progress to improve efficiency and avoid overloading the team. It promotes transparency, collaboration, and continuous improvement by visualizing work and enabling self-organization. Also it encourages teams to pull work into the system based on available capacity rather than pushing more work in. Explicit policies are established to guide how work should be handled and to ensure a smooth flow of tasks.
