What is Join?

Join is a project management and workflow system. It uses a visual board with columns representing different stages of work to provide a clear view of the workflow. Work items are represented as cards and their movement across the board indicates progress. Join focuses on limiting work in progress to improve efficiency and avoid overloading the team. It promotes transparency, collaboration, and continuous improvement by visualizing work and enabling self-organization. Also it encourages teams to pull work into the system based on available capacity rather than pushing more work in.

How to use it
1.
Visualise your tasks. Create a card for each task. In doing so, set the priority, time frame and subtasks and assign them to your contacts
2.
Move tasks across the board. As work progresses, move the task cards from left to right across the columns. For example, when a task is being actively worked on, move it to the "in progress" column. When it's completed, move it to the "Done" column.
3.
Track progress and visualize bottlenecks: Continuously monitor your Join board to see how tasks are flowing through the workflow. Identify any bottlenecks or areas where work is getting stuck. This visibility helps you identify and address issues promptly.
4.
Encourage a balanced workload by not overwhelming individuals with too many tasks. Ensure that work is evenly distributed and that team members can maintain a sustainable pace.
5.
Expand your team. Add new contacts via the contact area.
